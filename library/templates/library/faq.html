Frequently Asked Questions

What is LibraryWebApp?

LibraryWebApp is a website which allows you to find any book in just one place, in here.

How do I create an account?

Click register at the top right of the screen, enter your username, email, password, choose a profile picture(If you want to) and choose your role..

How do I search for a book?

Click search at the top of the screen, type in the book name/keywords in the search bar and click the search icon.

What is the Wishlist?

A whistlist allows you to put a book in "My Lists" which allows you to find it later when you want to read it.

How do I mark a book as read?

When you click on a book(view), you can cllick on "mark as read" to mark the book as read.

How do I leave a review?

In the book view, click on "+Add a review" then you can rate it and comment on the book then submit it.
